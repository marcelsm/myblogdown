About me

Hi, there! I am a researcher associate of the Aquatic Mammals Lab (LAMAq) research group, at Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina, Florianópolis, Brazil. I am most interested in behavioural ecology and the role of individual variation in behavior and it’s implications to the population level. I am also interested in how human populations interact with marine wildlife, and how both humans and animals can benefit from conservation. I had the opportunity to address such interesting topics using as a model the population of bottlenose dolphins that forages in cooperation with fishermen in Laguna, southern Brazil.
